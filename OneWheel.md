# OneWheel

## Speed and Body Position

Crouch to increase your speed by reducing drag. Leaning your center of gravity in front of the board is unsafe and should be avoided.

```
Safe Riding Posture:

  Side View:

  Upright (slow)       Crouched (fast)
       O                    O
      /|\                  /|\
      / \                 _/ \_
       |                   |
  ___[===]___          ___[===]___
```

```
Center of Gravity — Top View:

   ← FRONT →

  [  UNSAFE  ] ← leaning forward (nose dive risk)
  [ ← SAFE → ] ← balanced over board
  [  UNSAFE  ] ← leaning too far back (loss of control)
```

## Battery Management

Avoid pushing maximum speed when you're nearing the last 30% of your battery. Otherwise you risk the battery suddenly cutting out, which can cause a sudden nose-dive.

```
Battery Level Safety Zones:

  100% ██████████  Full — ride freely
   70% ███████░░░  Good — ride normally
   30% ███░░░░░░░  Caution — reduce speed
    0% ░░░░░░░░░░  Empty — board cuts out
```
