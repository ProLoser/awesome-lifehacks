## Shrink the Context and Scope
To achieve the highest quality results, it's imperative for you to focus on always shrinking the context and the scope of the task you assign the AI. 
by context, I am referring to the network or Internet of information you provide the AI to look through instead of being vague and generic provides specific files and line numbers and API names. 
by scope I mean the complexity of the task assigned to the AI. by giving it extremely explicit instructions for what you want, you'll increase your likeliness of a high quality result. avoid negative information because the AI does not have a concept of negative and positive and you are merely introducing more tokens that will affect its pattern recognition so use the tokens you do submit on focusing on what's correct and being specific.
